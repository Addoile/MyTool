int p_Orientation

SSTab Control - Determines whether the tabs are displayed on the top, bottom, left, or right of the SSTab control. This property may be one of the following:

  • SSTAB_OTOP
  • SSTAB_OBOTTOM
  • SSTAB_OLEFT
  • SSTAB_ORIGHT

Image - Determines how the picture and caption are oriented. This property may be one of the following:

  • PSPIC_OHORIZONTAL - caption (if any) is positioned just right of picture
  • PSPIC_OVERTICAL - caption (if any) is rotated 90 degrees clockwise and positioned just below picture

Applies To:

SSTab, Image

Categories:

SSTab Properties, Image Properties
