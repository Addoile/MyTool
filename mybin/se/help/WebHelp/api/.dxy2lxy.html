void _dxy2lxy(int scale_mode, int &x, int &y)

Converts the x and y positions given from pixels to the scale mode specified. scale_mode may be one of the constants SM_TWIP or SM_PIXEL defined in "slick.sh".

Parameters:

scale_mode - may be SM_PIXEL or SM_TWIP.

x - x coordinate

y - y coordinate

Category:

Miscellaneous Functions
