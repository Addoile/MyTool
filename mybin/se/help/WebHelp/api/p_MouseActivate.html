int p_MouseActivate

Determines how the click to get focus policy. This property may be one of the following values:

MA_DEFAULT
Action depends on the control.
MA_ACTIVATE
Active the form, give the control focus, and process a click event.
MA_ACTIVATEANDEAT
Active the form and give the control focus.
MA_NOACTIVATE
Do not active the form and process the click event
MA_NOACTIVATEANDEAT
Do not active the form.

Applies To:

All Window Objects

Category:

All Windows Properties
